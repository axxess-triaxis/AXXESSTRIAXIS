# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, safe, and productive project environment.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful, constructive, and professional.
- Assume good intent while still naming risks clearly.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Welcome thoughtful disagreement and evidence-based review.
- Be mindful that enterprise, public-sector, healthcare, NGO, and consulting users may have different operational needs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or hostile review language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing confidential, tenant-specific, or security-sensitive data in public channels.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to the project maintainers. Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, restrict participation, or escalate issues when needed to protect the project community.
