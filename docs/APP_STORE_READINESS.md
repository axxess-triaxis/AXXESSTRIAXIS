# App Store Readiness

AXXESS mobile is architecturally prepared for iOS beta builds, but App Store Connect credentials are required before TestFlight submission.

## Required Before TestFlight

- Apple Developer account.
- Bundle identifier: `com.triaxis.axxess`.
- App privacy labels.
- Privacy policy URL.
- Support URL.
- Store screenshots.
- App icon and splash assets.
- Account deletion initiation path.
- Sign in with Apple assessment if Apple OAuth or other social login is enabled.

## Current Status

- Expo app scaffold exists.
- EAS preview and production profiles exist.
- iOS prebuild path is documented.
- Signing credentials are the expected blocker.
