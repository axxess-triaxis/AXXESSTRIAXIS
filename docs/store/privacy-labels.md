# Apple Privacy Labels Draft

This draft supports App Store Connect privacy label preparation. Final answers must be confirmed by legal and product operations before submission.

## Data Categories

- Account identifiers: email address, display name, organization membership and role.
- User content: uploaded documents, selected email content, review notes, tasks, approvals, meetings and project updates.
- Usage data: workflow activity, audit events, AI review decisions, connector status, release readiness activity and product analytics events.
- Diagnostics: crash-free sessions, webview errors, API latency and release health evidence.

## Data Use

- App functionality: authentication, tenant access, workflow execution, knowledge retrieval, review approvals and audit history.
- Analytics: aggregate product usage, release readiness and pilot workflow health.
- Diagnostics: crash monitoring, build health, operational support and incident response.

## Linked To User

Account identifiers, workflow actions, audit events, review decisions and selected user content may be linked to a signed-in enterprise user for compliance and accountability.

## Tracking

AXXESS should not enable third-party tracking for store builds unless customer contracts and privacy disclosures explicitly allow it.

## Review Status

The Mobile Store Launch Console marks privacy labels as `needs_metadata` until the final App Store Connect declaration is approved.
