# Google Play Data Safety Draft

This draft supports Google Play Data Safety preparation. Final answers must be confirmed by legal and product operations before production release.

## Data Collected

- Personal information: email address, display name, role, department and organization.
- Files and documents: documents uploaded or selected by the organization.
- App activity: workflows, approvals, AI review decisions, audit events and navigation activity.
- Diagnostics: crash indicators, webview errors, release health checks and latency measurements.

## Purpose

- App functionality and account management.
- Enterprise workflow execution and tenant auditability.
- Security, fraud prevention, compliance and support operations.
- Analytics for product reliability and feature improvement.

## Sharing

AXXESS does not sell customer data. Data may be processed by configured infrastructure providers such as hosting, database, storage, analytics, email or identity providers under enterprise agreements.

## Security Practices

- Tenant-scoped access controls.
- Server-side session handling.
- Row Level Security for tenant tables.
- Audit logging for consequential actions.
- Human review before consequential AI output is applied.

## Deletion

Enterprise account and tenant deletion workflows are handled through admin and privacy request controls. Data retention depends on customer contract and compliance policy.
