## Capture Notes

- Use Demo Mode or investor preview for coherent North East Health Mission data.
- Keep browser width at 1440px for deck screenshots and 390px for mobile proof.
- Avoid capturing local dev tooling, console windows, or URL secrets.
- Provider-gated badges are intentional; they explain where production keys or integrations are required.
- Use the final guided demo step for pilot/waitlist/conversion screenshots.

## Website And Deck Use

- Dashboard and AI Workspace should be the first two product screenshots.
- Knowledge Hub and Approvals should support the governance/RAG story.
- Analytics and CRM should support enterprise operating-system positioning.
- Do not imply live production customer data unless the tenant is actually live and approved for display.
